1. Fruit Trees  
   - Apples (Apfelbaum)  
   - Pears (Birnenbaum)  
   - Cherries (Kirschbaum)  
   - Plums (Pflaumenbaum)  
   - Apricots (Aprikosenbaum)  

2. Nut Trees  
   - Walnut (Walnuss)  
   - Hazelnut (Haselnuss)  
   - Chestnut (Kastanie)

3. Flowering Trees
   - Wildflowers or Herbs (Kräuter)  
   - Pollinator Gardens (Bienengärten)

4. Berry Shrubs
   - Currant (Johannisbeere)  
   - Raspberry (Himbeere)  
   - Blueberry (Blaubeere)  

5. Deciduous Forest  
   - Oak (Eiche)  
   - Beech (Buche)  
   - Maple (Ahorn)  
   - Birch (Birke)  
   - Lime/Linden (Linde)  

6. Evergreen Forest  
   - Spruce (Fichte)  
   - Pine (Kiefer)  
   - Fir (Tanne)
